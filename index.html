About

A short introduction

I'm a product-minded professional with a decade of experience shipping considered, detail-first work — and an equally long-running habit of documenting the world around me through photography and writing.

Professionally, I care about clarity: simplifying complex problems into interfaces and systems people can actually use. Outside of work, I care about the opposite instinct — slowing down, noticing detail, and letting a place or a moment speak for itself before I try to explain it.

This site is a single home for both: a formal record of where I've worked, and an informal one of what I've noticed along the way.
